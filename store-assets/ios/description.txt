MedReminder helps you stay on top of your medications with reliable daily alarms, a clean health-focused design, and complete privacy — your data never leaves your device.

FEATURES
• Medication reminders with gentle audio tones and vibration
• Flexible schedules: Every Day, Once a Week, or Custom Days
• Daily dose tracking and adherence percentage
• Mark as Taken directly from the notification
• Snooze any reminder by 5 minutes
• Medication photos and icons
• Prescription notes (e.g. "take with food")
• Four alarm tones: Default, Gentle Bells, Morning Chime, Soft Beep
• Full backup and restore to a single file
• Works completely offline

PRIVACY
No account. No server. No analytics. Everything stays on your device.

DISCLAIMER
MedReminder is a reminder tool only. Always follow your healthcare provider's instructions.
