MedReminder helps you stay on top of your medications with reliable daily alarms, a clean health-focused design, and complete privacy — your data never leaves your device.

FEATURES

• Medication reminders — set one or multiple alarm times per medication, with gentle audio tones and vibration.
• Flexible schedules — choose Every Day, Once a Week, or Custom Days (pick any combination of days).
• Smart daily tracking — see which doses you've taken and your overall adherence percentage at a glance.
• Mark as Taken — tap directly from the notification or from inside the app.
• Snooze — bought yourself 5 more minutes? Snooze any reminder with one tap.
• Medication photos & icons — attach a photo of your pill pack or choose from built-in health icons.
• Notes — store special instructions like "take with food" alongside each medication.
• Alarm sounds — choose from four gentle tones: Default, Gentle Bells, Morning Chime, or Soft Beep.
• Backup & restore — export all your medications and logs to a single file and import them on any device.
• Works offline — no internet connection required after first install.

PRIVACY

MedReminder is private by design. There is no account to create, no server to connect to, and no analytics. Everything you enter — medications, schedules, photos, and logs — is stored exclusively on your device. Nothing is shared with anyone.

MEDICAL DISCLAIMER

MedReminder is a reminder tool, not a medical device. Always follow your doctor's or pharmacist's guidance for your medications.
