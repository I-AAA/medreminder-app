Privacy Policy

MedReminder – Medication Reminder App

Short version: MedReminder stores all your data exclusively on your own device. Nothing is collected, transmitted, or shared with any server, third party, or analytics service.

1. Who We Are

MedReminder is a personal medication reminder application. It is designed to help individuals track and remember their medication schedules. The app operates entirely on-device and has no back-end server, no user accounts, and no cloud infrastructure.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

The following data is stored locally on your device only, inside your browser's storage (localStorage and IndexedDB):

  • Your name and age (entered during onboarding)
  • The purpose for which you take medication
  • Medication names, dosages, schedules, and reminder times
  • Optional photos you attach to medications
  • Daily logs of doses marked as taken

This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted anywhere.

3. How We Use Your Information

Data you enter is used solely to:

  • Display personalised reminders and greetings within the app
  • Trigger alarm notifications at your configured times
  • Calculate your daily adherence statistics

4. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or disclose your data to any third party under any circumstances. There are no analytics, advertising networks, crash reporters, or tracking libraries integrated into this application.

5. Notifications

If you grant notification permission, the app uses your device's built-in notification system (managed by your operating system) to display medication reminders. No notification content is sent to any external server.

6. Photos

Photos you optionally attach to medications are compressed and stored in your device's IndexedDB storage. They are never uploaded, shared, or backed up externally unless you explicitly use the in-app "Export Backup" feature, which saves a file directly to your own device.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data persists until you clear your browser or app storage, uninstall the app, or use your device's "Clear Data" function. There is no account to delete because no account is ever created.

8. Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If the app is used on behalf of a child (e.g. by a parent or caregiver), all data remains on the caregiver's device and is governed by this same policy.

9. Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the revised version will be published at this URL with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Medical Disclaimer

MedReminder is a reminder tool only. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment recommendations. Always follow the instructions of your doctor, pharmacist, or other qualified healthcare provider regarding your medications and health.

Never disregard or delay seeking professional medical advice because of anything displayed in this application.

11. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please reach out via the app's listing page on the relevant app store.
